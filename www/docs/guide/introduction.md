# Introduction

Wox is an open-source, cross-platform quick launcher designed to boost your productivity.

## Key Features

- **App Launcher**: Find and launch applications with just a few keystrokes.
- **File Search**: Quickly locate files on your system.
- **Web Search**: Search Google, Bing, and more directly from the launcher.
- **Plugin Extensions**: Extend functionality with plugins like Calculator, Translator, Weather, etc.
- **Theme Customization**: Support for various themes to match your style.

## Why Wox?

Wox is more than just a launcher; it's a platform that can be infinitely extended via plugins. Whether you are a developer or a regular user, you'll find features that suit you.
